Yixiang Gao's Design Document

Interview Questions

Purpose

My goal is to create a portfolio website where, first, I can present my publications, projects, and other information that are related to my profession as a computer engineer researcher. Second, I also want to post articles that I consider would be useful for other users on my website. For example, my opinions regarding certain technologies, tutorials of how to use particular software or toolbox, and explainations on topics in the field that I study or interested in.

On a daily basis, I searched for information that could potentially help with projects that I worked on. However, often times it was hard to find the exact one article or document that would contain all of the information I needed. I ended up utilizing what I learned from multiple sources in order to make them useful. I believe this scenario happens not only just on me but many others. So I want the content from my website to be helpful to others who might work on a similar problem a I did.

My users can gain some pratical knowledge from the projects that I had done as well as experiences that I shared. Moreover, if people have questions on certain content either about technical details or the topic in general, it will also provide opportunities for potential better solutions and new ideas.

Use Cases

  1. People who are interested in the work that I am doing can look through the publications, articles, tutorials, code examples that are posted on my website.
  2. Someone who is working on a project in the same area might be able to find useful hands on tutorial and practical code examples to support him or her.
  3. People who are studying similar topics can find some useful explanations on certain topics. For example, some math operations are hard to understand by looking at just the equations. But once combined with figures, it becomes more intuitive immediately.

Design Concept

Audience

People who are either studying or working in the field of computer engineering with reasonable experiences of programming and developing different applications to solve real world problems. If they are still university students, they are most likely be junior, senior or graduate students. Also, the website is for those who are generally interested in the field of computer vision, machine learning, and robotics.

Rationale for organization

  • The main purpose for this organization is that users can get to their final destinations that they wanted within less than three clicks.
  • Each topic page corresponds to one of my use case to help my target audiences easily find the type of content they want.
  • The reason for this specific way of organization is that, first, audiences might want to know who I am and what I do to see whether this website might contain the content they need. Next, they can look through my publications to determine whether this is a reliable source. Finally they can enter into projects or tutorial to find their desired content.
